Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Mirmibug uses them, and your options regarding their use, in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including Mexican regulations.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to ensure websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide analytical information to website owners.

Cookies do not typically contain personally identifiable information. However, personal data that we store about you may be linked to information obtained from cookies.

2. How we use cookies

Mirmibug uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the proper operation of the website
  • To remember user preferences and settings
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • To improve our services, content, and user experience

We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal data without the user’s explicit consent.

3. Types of cookies we use

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly and cannot be disabled. They enable core functionality such as security, accessibility, and session management.

Analytical cookies

These cookies allow us to collect anonymous information about how visitors use the website, helping us improve performance and usability (e.g., pages visited, time on site, device type).

Functional cookies

These cookies remember choices you make, such as language or region, to provide a more personalized experience.

4. Third-party cookies

We may use third-party services (such as analytics or marketing tools) that place cookies on your device to help us analyze site usage.

These third parties operate under their own privacy and cookie policies, and Mirmibug does not control or assume responsibility for their practices.

5. Your cookie choices

When you first visit our website, you are presented with a cookie banner allowing you to accept the use of cookies.

You may also manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

6. How to disable or delete cookies

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. Instructions vary depending on your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.).

For more information, please consult your browser’s official support documentation.

7. Legal compliance

This Cookie Policy is designed to comply with applicable Mexican data protection laws, including the Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (LFPDPPP), as well as international best practices.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy at any time to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
